FAQS

Why The X-Championship Competition?

The X-Championship is a fun e-sports event that aims to provide game lovers with a very different experience from the norm, promote cohesion among young people, and create a healthy foundation for businesses to connect and thrive with their audiences and contribute to the well-being of people in the community

Helping To Shape Social Responsibility

The X Championship Competition does not just aim to make a profit at the expense of the community. It considers how to give back to people, because it aims to connect people and promote cohesion. Therefore, part of the proceeds from the competition goes to the community to give something back. In addition, each winner will offer a fully funded scholarship to a child from the slum each season of the competition. The X Championship hopes to carry out other humanitarian activities over the years to contribute to the development of society.
